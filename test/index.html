¶8.2 有误

¶8.2.1 等式未断行:

$$
\begin{equation} \label{eq2}
\begin{aligned}
a &= b + c \\
&= d + e + f + g \\
&= h + i \\
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
$$

¶8.2.2 方程未断行不支持多行编号:

$$
\begin{align}
a &= b + c \label{eq3} \\
x &= yz \\
l &= m - n \\
\end{align}
$$

¶8.2.3 多列式未断行不编号:

$$
\begin{align}
-4 + 5x &= 2+y \\
w+2 &= -1+w \\
ab &= cb \\
\end{align}
$$

¶8.2.4 矩阵未断行：

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & a_1 & a_1^2 & \cdots & a_1^n \\
1 & a_2 & a_2^2 & \cdots & a_2^n \\
\vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
1 & a_m & a_m^2 & \cdots & a_m^n \\
\end{pmatrix}
$$
